Welcome to RLCSdle!

An RL Esports game where you guess the team & event from statistics and other info.

How to Play

Select a season, event, and team from the dropdowns that matches the information given.

Information is revealed as follows:

  1. Series results, game scores, player scoreboard stats
  2. Event stage names, series casters, first letter of opposing teams, first letter of coach
  3. Length of opposing team names, first letter of mystery team's lowest scoring player (by total points)
  4. Full opposing team names, first letter of mystery team's middle scoring player
  5. First letter of highest scoring player, dates of series, coach's country
  6. Full coach name, players' countries
